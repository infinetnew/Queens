👑 Queens — Official Guide

🎯 Objective
Place 10 Queens on the 10×10 grid without breaking any rule.

♟️ Rules
• 1 Queen per row
• 1 Queen per column
• 1 Queen per colored region
• Queens cannot touch each other, not even diagonally

⚡ Smart Board
When you place a Queen, impossible cells are automatically blocked.
Use logic and elimination to discover the remaining valid positions.

💡 Hints (max 3)
• Remove incorrect Queen → +30s
• Insert correct Queen → +45s
• Full correction → +60s
Hints help fix the board but increase your final time.

⏱ Time
Your final time includes solving time and hint penalties.
Lower times rank higher in the daily leaderboard.

🧩 Daily Puzzle
Every day a new global puzzle is released.
All players solve the same board and have only one attempt.

🎚️ Puzzle Difficulty
Each puzzle can be Easy, Medium or Hard.
Harder puzzles require deeper logical reasoning.

👑 Royal Score
Completing puzzles awards Royal Score:
Easy → 5 points
Medium → 20 points
Hard → 50 points
These points determine the all-time Royal Ranking.

🏆 Leaderboards
Queens includes multiple rankings:
• Daily leaderboard (fastest times)
• Monthly leaderboard (season points)
• Country leaderboard (top players by nation)
• Royal Ranking (all-time score)

📊 Monthly Competition
Daily results award monthly points:
1st → 15 • 2nd → 10 • 3rd → 7
4th–10th → 3 • Participation → 1

🌍 Country Ranking
Each month the top 3 players of every country contribute points.
Their combined score determines the global country ranking.

⚔ Challenges
After solving a puzzle you can launch a challenge.
Share your time and see if others can beat it.

🏅 Achievements
Unlock badges for speed, logic, consistency and competition.
Some achievements are extremely rare.

👤 Player Profiles
Each player has statistics, achievements and performance rating.
You can also view achievements of other players.

⚔ Nearby Rivals
The Royal Ranking shows players directly above and below you.
Climb the leaderboard by surpassing them.

📲 App Features
Install Queens for faster access and notifications.

👑 Philosophy
Queens does not reward luck.
It rewards clarity, precision and consistency.
